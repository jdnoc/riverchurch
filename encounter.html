Speakers

Pastor Jeremiah Thomas

Pastor Jeremiah Thomas and his wife Pastor Julia Thomas started River Church in Brockport, NY 3 years ago. They were ordained at Revival Ministries International under Dr. Rodney Howard-Browne.

His goal is to bring revival and restoration to all of western NY, starting with Brockport. His spirit filled, bible based messages educate, challenge, and edify all who listen.

Evangelist Jana Pauls

Missionary and Evangelist Jana Pauls has been in the full time ministry since graduating Bible School in 2010 with a Bachelors in Theology Degree. She was licensed as a minister in 2012 through Revival Ministries International under Drs. Rodney and Adonica Howard-Browne.

She travels internationally to preach and teach the Word of God and train and mobilise believers to bring in the harvest of souls. She has ministered across America, Europe, Africa, Asia and the Middle East in a total of 30 nations so far and counting.
